There are numerous ways authors can purvey propaganda into their news stories, such techniques can involve appealing to the emotions, irrelevant uses of it statistics and quotes, and the use of ambiguous language to divert the reader from specific issues. This research project has investigated ways to automate the identification propaganda by producing distance metrics between publishers of the 4 categories: the style of writing, the use of emotive language, the use of facts and the use of ambiguous language. To do this, $ARTICLES_COUNT$ articles have been analysed and grouped into $EVENTS_COUNT$ different events, and for each event a distance metric for each category listed above was produced relative to each event.
